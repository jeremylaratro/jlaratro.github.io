ELECTRONICS LAB

// Workbench Setup

OVERVIEW

My electronics workbench setup for hardware projects, PCB work, and RF experimentation. A well-organized workspace is essential for efficient prototyping and repair work.

EQUIPMENT

The workbench includes essential tools for electronics work: soldering station, multimeter, oscilloscope, power supplies, and various hand tools. Organization is key when working with small components and complex circuits.

CAPABILITIES

  • PCB prototyping and assembly
  • SMD and through-hole soldering
  • Circuit debugging and analysis
  • RF testing and measurement
  • Firmware development and flashing
  • Hardware repair and restoration
